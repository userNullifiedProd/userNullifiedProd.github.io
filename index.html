About us

We are a team of 3, bringing you Minecraft servers for our custom modpacks.

It all started as a small side projects for friends, but then we thought, why not let other people in ?

And here we are today, thanks to everyone in our community.

Stability

Our servers are quite stable. Of course, anything can happen, our servers notify us, if anything bad is happening, so we can get on and fix it.

Speed

Nobody wants to play on a laggy server. Our servers are optimized to the best of our abilities, to provide as little lag as possible.

Experience

An experienced Admin Team is a key to a good server. Our Admin Team can help you with any problem you might have with our servers, just ask!
